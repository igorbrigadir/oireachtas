Diarmaid O Cadhla
Independent

Sitting TD : No

Main message to voters:

Decisions are made in our name, but we have no say - we are deceived & bullied . Our country has been sold-out, the wishes of the people count for nothing, there is no respect. It is not a 'left' or 'right' issue, we must assert our right to Democratic Representation, it's about our DEMOCRACY!

Election history:

Stood in General Election 2011. I established Ionad an Phobail (Community Resource Centre) in Cork and provide professional advice about Welfare, Housing and Employment Rights. I have led the work of The People's Convention working for Democratic Renewal in Ireland.

Priorities :

  1. We have a right to be CONSULTED about the decisions being made in our name, and to be part of making those decisions - to be represented!
  2. End the Political Party 'whip', it is anti-democratic and obstructs the representation of the Constituents, who each TD is duty bound to represent.
  3. Defend our SOVEREIGN and DEMOCRATIC rights as a people, our resources are given away, our nation sold-out . We must assert our national self-interest.

Processing of technical information on website

When using our website, you can decide whether you wish to accept or decline these cookies by modifying the settings in your browser.
